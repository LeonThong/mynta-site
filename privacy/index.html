Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Mynta ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal finance app developed by Leon Thong, an independent developer based in Singapore. This policy explains what data we collect, what we don't, and how we handle your information.

🔒 The short version

Your financial data (transactions, budgets, savings goals, amounts, categories, and notes) is stored locally on your device. We never see it, store it, or send it to our servers.

What we never collect

✕Your transaction amounts or balances
✕Your budget limits or savings targets
✕Your custom categories, notes, or labels
✕Your bank credentials. We never ask for them
✕We never sell your personal or financial data. Anonymous analytics are shared with trusted services (see Third-party services below)

What we collect anonymously

We use privacy-focused analytics to understand how the app is used so we can improve it. This data is anonymous and never linked to your identity.

✓Which screens and features you visit
✓Which default categories you use (e.g. Food, Transport, not custom ones you create)
✓How many goals you create and their type (not names or amounts)
✓App crashes and error reports
✓Device type and OS version
✓Subscription status (via Apple)

Third-party services

Mynta uses the following third-party services:

  • PostHog: anonymous product analytics. PostHog Privacy Policy
  • Sentry: crash reporting. Sentry Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: subscription management via Apple's App Store. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

None of these services receive your financial data.

Cloud backup

If you choose to back up your data in a future version, backups will use your own iCloud or Google Drive account. We never see, access, or store your backup data. Backups are encrypted by the platform provider.

Data storage

All financial data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. If you delete the app, your data is deleted. We have no server-side copy of your information and cannot recover it.

Children's privacy

Mynta is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at hello@mynta.finance.

Your money is personal. We keep it that way.
