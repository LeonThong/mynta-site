Frequently asked questions

Is Mynta really free?

Yes, completely. Mynta includes expense logging, budgets, savings goals, recurring transactions, and full history — no paywalls, no limits, and no ads.

Where is my data stored?

On your device only. We don't have servers that store your financial data. If you delete the app, your data is gone and we can't recover it. Cloud backup via iCloud/Google Drive is planned for a future update.

Can I sync between devices?

Not yet. This is planned for a future update. Your data currently lives on one device.

Do you track or sell my spending?

Never. Your transactions, amounts, and notes stay on your phone and are never sent to us. We collect only anonymous, non-identifying usage analytics to improve the app — never your financial data. See the privacy policy for the full picture.

I lost my data after reinstalling. Can you help?

Unfortunately, since all data is stored locally on your device, we cannot recover it after the app is deleted. We recommend waiting for the cloud backup feature in a future update before reinstalling.
